Sinigang Recipe

Description

Sinigang is a popular Filipino sour soup made with meat (usually pork) and a variety of vegetables in a tangy broth.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly, cut into cubes
  • 14 cup fish sauce
  • 1 packet sinigang mix
  • 3 medium tomatoes, cut into wedges
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1 cup radish, sliced
  • 1 cup string beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 cup eggplant, sliced
  • 1 cup water spinach (kangkong)
  • 2-3 pieces long green chili peppers (siling haba)
  • 1 liter water

Steps

  1. In a pot, combine pork belly, fish sauce, and water. Bring to a boil and simmer for 30 minutes.
  2. Add the sinigang mix, tomatoes, and onion. Simmer for another 10 minutes.
  3. Add the radish, string beans, and eggplant. Cook for 5-7 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Add the water spinach and long green chili peppers. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with steamed rice.

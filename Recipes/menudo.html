Menudo Recipe

Description

Menudo is a popular Filipino stew made with meat (usually beef or pork) that is cooked in a flavorful tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork shoulder, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1/2 tsp black ground peppercorns
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/8 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate the pork cubes in the soy sauce and lemon juice for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat the cooking oil in a pot and sauté the garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add the marinated pork and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Add the tomato sauce, tomato paste, peppercorns, and water. Bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes or until the pork is tender.
  6. Season with salt to taste and serve hot.

Adobo Recipe

Description

Adobo is a popular Filipino dish made with meat (usually pork) that is marinated in a mixture of vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and other spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate the pork cubes in the soy sauce and vinegar for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat the cooking oil in a pot and sauté the garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add the marinated pork and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Add the bay leaves, peppercorns, and water. Bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes or until the pork is tender.
  6. Season with salt to taste and serve hot.
